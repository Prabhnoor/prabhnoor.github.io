Scholar

I truly believe that behind every moment in life, there is a hidden meaning, a meaning that is deeper than we can see at first glance. In my life, have always led me to conclude the same thing: hard work is always rewarded, and that one should show compassion towards those in need regardless of the circumstance. Essentially, the main drive behind all ventures, and most actions is a distaste for the status quo. In some cases it’s a desire to be different, in other cases it is a need to do something novel. Either way, it begins with a fundamental idea that what is, could be better. For me, it’s a desire to help others that fuels much of what I do, while making sure the future is the best it can be.
